Vision Statement - eBundleMaster

The aim of this project is to design,develop and implement an e-ticketing system that allows customers to purchase concert and performance tickets, select date and location of concert, purchase ticket.In addtion the system offers bundles to customers which allows them to search for transport such as bus and train, hotel room and restaurant.

Fully Dressed Use Case:

1. Account Sign-up

Use Case Name:Account Sign-Up
Use Case Id 1
Priority 1
Source
Primary Business Actory Customer
Other Participating Actors Database Administrator
Description A customer needs to create an account to in order to purchase tickets from the E-ticketing system.
Preconditions A customer must hold a valid email address
Trigger
Typical Scenario
  1. A customer requests a sign up for the EbundleSystem.
  2. The system prepares a form for sign up
  3. The customer inputs their email address, a username and a strong password (Mixed case, characters, letters and numbers)
  4. The system sends a confirmation email to the user's email address
  5. The system tells the customer to check their email address to verify their account with EbundleSystem
  6. The customer goes to their email, clicks on the link to validate their email
  7. The customer goes into the EbundleSystem website and requests to log in
ConclusionsA customer creates an account with EbundleSystem
Post ConditionsA customer can only create one account with one email address
Business RulesA customer can only create one account with one email address
Implementation Constraints
